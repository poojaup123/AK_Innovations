1. System Overview & Architecture

üéØ What is Factory Management System?

A comprehensive manufacturing ERP solution designed for small to medium enterprises. The system integrates all aspects of manufacturing operations from procurement to production, with advanced accounting automation and complete material traceability.

üîë Key Capabilities

  • Real-time Inventory: Multi-state tracking (Raw, WIP, Finished, Scrap)
  • BOM-driven Production: Material planning with cost calculations
  • Accounting Automation: Double-entry bookkeeping with GST compliance
  • Batch Tracking: Complete traceability from supplier to customer
  • Advanced Reports: PO fulfillment, vendor aging, cost analysis

5. 3-Step GRN Workflow (Complete Guide)

üéØ Why 3-Step Workflow?

The 3-step workflow separates material receipt, invoice processing, and payment - providing better financial control, audit trails, and compliance with accounting standards.

üìà Complete Workflow Overview

Step 1: GRN Creation (Material Receipt)
üì¶ Goods received ‚Üí Inventory updated ‚Üí GRN Clearing Account used
Dr. Inventory Account
Cr. GRN Clearing Account
Step 2: Invoice Processing
üìÑ Vendor invoice received ‚Üí Liability created ‚Üí Clearing account cleared
Dr. GRN Clearing Account (base amount)
Dr. GST Input Tax (tax amount)
Dr. Freight Account (if applicable)
Cr. Vendor Payable Account (total)
Step 3: Payment Processing
üí∞ Payment made ‚Üí Vendor liability cleared ‚Üí Cash/Bank reduced
Dr. Vendor Payable Account
Cr. Bank/Cash Account

11. Troubleshooting & FAQ

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

General System Questions

Q: Can I modify a GRN after it's created?
A: GRNs can be modified before invoice processing. Once an invoice is linked, modifications require reversing the workflow steps.
Q: What happens if I receive partial quantity against a PO?
A: Create GRN for received quantity. System tracks pending quantities and allows multiple GRNs against single PO.
Q: How do I handle invoice amount different from GRN amount?
A: During invoice processing, you can adjust amounts. System handles price variances automatically through appropriate accounts.
Q: Can I process partial payments?
A: Yes, payment allocation allows partial payments. System tracks outstanding amounts and payment history.

Workflow-Specific Questions

Q: What if vendor invoice is delayed?
A: Material remains in inventory, but liability is in GRN Clearing Account until invoice is processed. This provides better cash flow visibility.
Q: How to handle returns or rejections?
A: Create return GRN with negative quantities. System reverses inventory and accounting entries appropriately.
