Stock Valuation Methods:
  • FIFO: First items purchased are first to be consumed
  • LIFO: Last items purchased are first to be consumed
  • Moving Average: Average cost of all items in stock
Batch Tracking:

Enable to track materials from purchase to finished goods. Essential for quality control and traceability.

Allows items to be purchased in one unit (kg) and consumed in another (pcs). Useful for manufacturing environments.
