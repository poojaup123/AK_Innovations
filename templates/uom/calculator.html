How It Works
  1. Select an item that has UOM conversion configured
  2. Enter the quantity you want to convert
  3. Choose the "From" unit (what you have)
  4. Choose the "To" unit (what you want)
  5. Click Calculate to see the result
Note: Only conversions configured for each specific item are available.
