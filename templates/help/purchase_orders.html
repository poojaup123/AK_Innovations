Purchase Orders Guide

Complete guide to managing supplier purchases

Overview

Purchase Orders (PO) manage the procurement of materials, tools, and services from suppliers. They integrate with inventory, accounting, and GRN systems for complete procurement workflow.

Creating Purchase Orders

Supplier Selection: Choose from registered suppliers or add new ones as needed
Item Selection: Add items with quantities, rates, and specifications
Terms & Conditions: Include payment terms, delivery schedule, and quality requirements
Tax Calculation: GST is automatically calculated based on item HSN codes and supplier GST registration

Goods Receipt (GRN)

When materials arrive:

  1. Create GRN: Record what was actually received
  2. Quality Check: Inspect materials and record quality status
  3. Update Inventory: Stock levels are automatically updated
  4. Invoice Matching: Match supplier invoice with GRN
  5. Payment Processing: Generate payment vouchers

Cost Tracking

Purchase orders track various costs:

  • Item Cost: Base price of materials
  • Transportation: Freight and delivery charges
  • GST: Input tax credit tracking
  • Other Charges: Handling, insurance, etc.

Required Information

Supplier Details:
  • Company name and address
  • GST number
  • Contact person and phone
  • Payment terms
Item Details:
  • Item code and description
  • Quantity and unit of measure
  • Rate and total amount
  • Delivery schedule
Important: Always verify supplier information and maintain updated supplier master data for accurate GST compliance and payment processing.
