Accounting System Guide

Complete Tally-like accounting system for manufacturing

Overview

The integrated accounting system provides complete financial management with automatic double-entry bookkeeping, GST compliance, and seamless integration with all manufacturing operations.

Chart of Accounts

Pre-configured account groups following Indian accounting standards:

Assets:
  • Current Assets (Cash, Bank, Inventory)
  • Fixed Assets (Plant, Machinery, Equipment)
  • Investments
Liabilities:
  • Current Liabilities (Creditors, Loans)
  • Capital (Owner's Equity)
  • Provisions
Income:
  • Sales (Local, Interstate, Export)
  • Other Income
  • Interest Income
Expenses:
  • Purchase (Raw Materials, Consumables)
  • Manufacturing Expenses
  • Administrative Expenses

Automatic Integration

All business transactions automatically create proper accounting entries:

  • Purchase Orders: Create creditor entries and inventory valuation
  • Sales Orders: Generate debtor entries and revenue recognition
  • Job Work: Track work-in-progress and processing costs
  • Production: Calculate cost of goods manufactured
  • Payments: Update cash/bank and creditor/debtor balances

GST Management

Tax Rates

Automatic GST calculation based on HSN codes

Returns

GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B report generation

Input Credit

Track and reconcile input tax credit

Financial Reports

Primary Reports:
  • Trial Balance
  • Profit & Loss Statement
  • Balance Sheet
  • Cash Flow Statement
Management Reports:
  • Outstanding Receivables
  • Outstanding Payables
  • Inventory Valuation
  • Cost of Goods Sold

Payment Management

Complete payment processing system:

Receipt Vouchers: Record customer payments and adjust receivables
Payment Vouchers: Record supplier payments and adjust payables
Bank Reconciliation: Match bank statements with book entries
TDS Management: Calculate and track tax deductions at source

Manufacturing Cost Accounting

Specialized features for manufacturing:

  • Material Cost: Track raw material consumption by product
  • Labor Cost: Allocate direct and indirect labor costs
  • Overhead Allocation: Distribute manufacturing overheads
  • Work-in-Progress: Value inventory at different production stages
  • Cost Centers: Department-wise cost tracking

Data Security & Backup

Important:
  • All financial data is automatically backed up
  • Audit trails are maintained for all transactions
  • User permissions control access to sensitive data
  • Regular data validation prevents inconsistencies
Pro Tip: The system maintains complete audit trails and supports easy integration with external accounting software like Tally for comprehensive financial management.
